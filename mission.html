Our Vision

We see STEM as a gateway to higher education and future opportunities, and we are committed to providing the resources and support needed to ensure every student can access and excel in math across the United States.

Mission Statement

Stem For Others is a certified 501(c)(3) nonprofit committed to educating underserved youth by offering mentorship, tutoring, and supplemental resources in science, engineering, technology, and math (STEM).
